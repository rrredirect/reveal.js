The screen can be a portal into the real world. A gateway to the "things and forces which govern the universe."

  • Children are living in a world of screens. We can use what they are most used to, in order to show them an alternative.
  • We are showcasing engaging screen-based content that is not addictive. This may be a new experience for those born into the attention economy.
  • Allows for interactivity and unique ways to explain scientific concepts.
  • If used carefully and sparingly, the screen can become a successful combination of many different educational technologies.
  • Presentation gives context to children about the scientific and artistic experience they are about to have in the real world.
